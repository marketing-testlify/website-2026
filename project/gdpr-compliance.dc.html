GDPR Compliance

Last updated: July 2026

Testlify is built to help you hire in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Here's how we protect personal data and support your compliance.

1. Lawful, fair processing

We process personal data only on documented instructions and for clearly defined purposes — providing assessment, interviewing and related Services.

2. Data subject rights

We help you honour data-subject requests, including the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict and port personal data, and to object to processing.

3. Data protection by design

Privacy is built into the product: encryption in transit and at rest, granular access controls, and data-minimisation by default.

4. International transfers

Cross-border transfers rely on approved mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses. See our Data Privacy Framework.

5. Sub-processors & DPA

We engage vetted sub-processors under strict terms and offer a Data Processing Agreement to all customers.

6. Certifications

Testlify maintains SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications. Learn more in our Security Practices and GDPR FAQs.
