CCPA Compliance

Last updated: July 2026

Testlify supports compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and respects the privacy rights of California residents.

1. We do not sell personal data

Testlify does not sell personal information. We process data solely to provide and improve the Services.

2. Your CCPA rights

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used;
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information;
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data);
  • The right not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

3. How to exercise your rights

Submit a request through Contact us. We will verify your identity and respond within the timeframes required by law.

4. Categories of information

We collect account, billing, assessment and usage data as described in our Privacy Policy.

This is a plain-language summary for this recreation. For definitive CCPA terms, please refer to official Testlify documentation.
