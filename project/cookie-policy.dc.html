This Cookie Policy explains how Testlify uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our websites and use our Services.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit — such as your preferences and login state — to improve your experience.

2. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

Required for the site to function — authentication, security and core features. These cannot be switched off.

Analytics cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. All analytics data is aggregated.

Preference cookies

Remember choices you make, such as language and region, to personalise your experience.

Marketing cookies

Used to measure the effectiveness of campaigns and show relevant content. Set only with your consent.

3. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings, and manage consent through our cookie banner. Disabling some cookies may affect how the site works.
