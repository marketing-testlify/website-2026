Our story.

From a small idea to a global hiring platform

A small team on a mission to make hiring better for everyone. In 2021, our founders saw a problem that every recruiter, hiring manager, and candidate felt: hiring was broken — slow, biased, inconsistent, and overwhelmingly manual.

There had to be a better way. So we built Testlify, an intelligent assessment and interviewing platform designed to cut through the noise with clarity, fairness, and speed.

What started as a small bootstrapped idea has grown into a team of 100+ thoughtful humans across the world, serving thousands of HR professionals every day.
