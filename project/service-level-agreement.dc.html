Service Level Agreement

Last updated: July 2026

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") describes the availability commitment and support standards Testlify provides to eligible customers.

1. Uptime commitment

Testlify targets 99.9% monthly uptime for the core Services, measured excluding scheduled maintenance and events outside our reasonable control.

2. Scheduled maintenance

We aim to perform maintenance during low-traffic windows and to give advance notice of any planned downtime that may affect the Services.

3. Support response times

  • Critical (Service down): initial response within 1 business hour;
  • High (major feature impaired): within 4 business hours;
  • Normal (general issues): within 1 business day.

4. Status & monitoring

We monitor the platform continuously and publish incident updates. Enterprise customers receive additional monitoring and escalation options.

5. Service credits

Where we fall short of the uptime commitment in a given month, eligible customers may request service credits in line with their agreement.

This is a plain-language summary for this recreation. For the definitive SLA governing the live product, please refer to the official Testlify document.
