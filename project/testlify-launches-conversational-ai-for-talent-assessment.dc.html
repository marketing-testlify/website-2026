Testlify launches conversational AI for talent assessment

TL
Testlify Team
Product
·4 min read·July 2026

Today we're launching conversational AI across Testlify — bringing automated, scored video, audio and chat interviews to the platform teams already use to assess skills. It's the biggest step yet toward our mission: helping hiring teams stop guessing and start proving who can actually do the job.

"Every candidate deserves a fair, consistent conversation. Now every team can offer one — at any scale."

What's new

Conversational AI interviews let you screen and interview candidates automatically, in the format that fits the role:

  • Video interviews for roles where presence and communication matter.
  • Audio interviews for quick, low-friction spoken screening.
  • Chat interviews for asynchronous, text-based assessment at scale.

Every interview is scored against a structured rubric, so candidates are compared like-for-like — no drift between reviewers, no unstructured gut calls.

Why it matters

Interviews are where hiring is slowest and most inconsistent. By automating the early rounds and scoring them objectively, teams reclaim hours, widen their funnels, and keep every decision defensible — while candidates get a faster, fairer experience.

Available now

Conversational AI is rolling out across Testlify plans today, on top of the 3,500+ validated tests, AI resume screening and 100+ ATS integrations you already know. One platform, from application to offer.
