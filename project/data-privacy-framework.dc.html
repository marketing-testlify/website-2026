Data Privacy Framework

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how Testlify handles international data transfers and the frameworks we rely on to keep personal data protected wherever it flows.

1. Cross-border transfers

When personal data is transferred outside its region of origin, we ensure an equivalent level of protection using approved legal mechanisms.

2. Standard Contractual Clauses

For transfers from the EEA, UK and Switzerland, Testlify relies on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and applicable addenda.

3. Safeguards

  • Encryption in transit and at rest;
  • Access controls and least-privilege principles;
  • Vendor due diligence and contractual data-protection terms;
  • Continuous monitoring and audit.

4. Sub-processors

We maintain a current list of sub-processors, each bound by obligations equivalent to our Data Processing Agreement.

5. Certifications

Testlify holds SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications and complies with GDPR and CCPA. See Security Practices.
