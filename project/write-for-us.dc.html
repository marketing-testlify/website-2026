Submission guidelines

  • Original & well-researched: fresh, informative content not copied from other sources. AI content will not be tolerated.
  • Audience-focused: write with HR professionals, recruiters, and talent managers in mind.
  • Length: articles should be between 1,200 and 1,500 words.
  • Formatting: clear headings (H1, H2), structured well for easy readability.
  • External link: include one relevant link to a credible external source.

What we don't accept

  • Salesy content: should inform and educate, not just promote products or services.
  • Plain AI content: AI-generated and humanized AI content will not be considered.
  • Plagiarism: only original content that hasn't been published elsewhere.
  • Inaccurate or misleading info: fact-check everything; all data must be accurate and reliable.
  • Overly technical or too simple: keep it relevant to HR professionals, talent managers, and recruiters.
