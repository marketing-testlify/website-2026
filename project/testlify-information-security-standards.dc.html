Information security standards.

The standards
we hold ourselves to

A summary of the frameworks, controls and commitments that define information security at Testlify.

SOC 2 Type II

Audited controls for security, availability, and confidentiality, reviewed annually.

ISO/IEC 27001

A certified information security management system (ISMS) governing our controls.

Data protection

GDPR and CCPA compliance with DPAs and approved transfer mechanisms.

Encryption standards

TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest, with managed key rotation.

Access & identity

SSO, MFA, least-privilege access and periodic access reviews.

Resilience

Automated backups, tested disaster recovery and high-availability architecture.
