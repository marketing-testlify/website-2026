Fair Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to feel confident using Testlify. This Fair Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are handled — written to be genuinely fair, not full of fine print.

1. Free trial first

We offer a free trial so you can experience the platform before you pay. We encourage you to use it fully to make sure Testlify is right for your team.

2. Monthly plans

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time and will not renew for the following month. We do not provide partial refunds for the current month once it has begun, but you keep access until the period ends.

3. Annual plans

If you purchase an annual plan and are not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund, provided the account has not had significant usage. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable but remain active for the full term.

4. Per-candidate credits

Unused candidate credits may be eligible for a refund at our discretion where they have not been consumed. Consumed assessments are non-refundable.

5. How to request a refund

Email our team or reach out through Contact us with your account details. We aim to respond within 3 business days.

This is a plain-language summary for this recreation. For the definitive refund terms governing the live product, please refer to the official Testlify Fair Refund Policy.
