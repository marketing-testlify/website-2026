GDPR FAQs

Last updated: July 2026

Common questions about how Testlify handles personal data under GDPR. Still need help? Contact our team.

Is Testlify GDPR compliant?

Yes. We process personal data lawfully, offer a Data Processing Agreement, maintain SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications, and support all data-subject rights.

Who is the controller and who is the processor?

Your organisation is the controller and determines why data is processed. Testlify is the processor, acting on your documented instructions.

Where is my data stored?

Data is hosted with reputable cloud providers using strong security controls. For transfers outside the EEA we rely on approved mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

How do candidates exercise their rights?

Candidates can request access, correction or deletion of their data. We assist controllers in responding to these requests promptly.

Do you sign DPAs?

Yes — our Data Processing Agreement is available to all customers.

How long is data retained?

Only as long as needed to provide the Services or meet legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted.
