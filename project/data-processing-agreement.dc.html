Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: July 2026

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between Testlify ("Processor") and the customer ("Controller") and governs the processing of personal data under GDPR and other applicable laws.

1. Roles

The Controller determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. Testlify acts as Processor, processing personal data only on the Controller's documented instructions.

2. Scope of processing

Testlify processes candidate and user personal data to provide assessment, interviewing and related Services, for the duration of the agreement.

3. Sub-processors

The Controller authorises Testlify to engage vetted sub-processors under written terms that impose data-protection obligations equivalent to this DPA. A current list is available on request.

4. Security measures

Testlify implements appropriate technical and organisational measures, including encryption, access controls and monitoring, aligned to SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001. See our Security Practices.

5. International transfers

Where personal data is transferred across borders, Testlify relies on approved transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses. See our Data Privacy Framework.

6. Data subject rights & breach

Testlify assists the Controller in responding to data-subject requests and will notify the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal-data breach.
